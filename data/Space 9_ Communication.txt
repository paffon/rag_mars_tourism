*Communication*
How can I stay in touch with family and friends?
Passengers can stay in touch with family and friends through email and video calls, subject to communication delays.
Is there communication with Earth during the trip?
Yes, communication with Earth is available, but subject to significant delays due to the vast distance.
What are the communication delays?
Communication delays can range from several minutes to over 20 minutes, depending on the relative positions of Earth and Mars.
What communication devices are provided?
Communication devices are provided for passenger use.

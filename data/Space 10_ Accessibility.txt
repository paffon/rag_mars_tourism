*Accessibility*
Are the trips accessible to people with disabilities?
We are committed to making our Mars trips as accessible as possible. However, the unique environment of space travel and Mars presents challenges. We conduct thorough assessments of individual needs and provide detailed information about potential limitations. We work closely with accessibility experts to develop accommodations and support services.
What accommodations are available?
We offer a range of accommodations, including modified cabins with accessible features, specialized mobility aids, and personalized assistance from trained crew members. We also provide sensory-friendly environments and communication support. Pre-trip evaluations are conducted to determine necessary accommodations.
What are the crew to passenger ratios?
We maintain a high crew-to-passenger ratio, with dedicated personnel trained in accessibility support. This ensures that passengers with disabilities receive personalized assistance and care throughout the trip. Our crew includes medical professionals, accessibility specialists, and support staff.

*Luggage & Personal Items*
What is the baggage allowance?
The baggage allowance is limited due to space constraints. Passengers can bring a specified amount of personal items.
What items are prohibited on the spacecraft?
Prohibited items include hazardous materials, weapons, and illegal substances. A detailed list of prohibited items will be provided.
Can I bring my pet?
Due to the unique environment and safety considerations, pets are not allowed on the spacecraft.
What electronics can I bring?
Passengers can bring personal electronics, such as laptops and cameras, subject to approval.
How are personal items stored?
Personal items are stored in designated storage compartments within the cabins.
What is the shipping policy for items to mars?
Shipping personal items to mars is possible, but subject to strict size, weight, and regulation restrictions.

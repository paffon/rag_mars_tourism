*Currency and Finances*
What currency is used on Mars?
A digital currency, "Martian Credits," is used on Mars for transactions. Earth based currency can be exchanged once on Mars. The exchange rate is determined by the Martian Central Bank and is subject to fluctuations.
Are there banking facilities on Mars?
Yes, banking facilities are available on Mars, allowing passengers to manage their finances. These facilities offer services such as currency exchange, account management, and loan services.
What is the cost of living on Mars?
The cost of living on Mars varies depending on individual needs and preferences. We provide detailed information on living expenses during the pre-trip briefing. This includes information on housing, food, transportation, and entertainment.

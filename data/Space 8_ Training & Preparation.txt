*Training & Preparation*
Is pre-flight training required?
Yes, pre-flight training is mandatory for all passengers. This training prepares passengers for the unique challenges of space travel.
What does the training involve?
Training involves physical conditioning, spaceflight simulations, and emergency procedures.
How long is the training?
The training duration varies depending on the trip package and individual needs.
Are there any fitness requirements?
Yes, passengers must meet specific fitness requirements to ensure their ability to handle the physical demands of space travel.
What are the psychological evaluations?
Psychological evaluations are conducted to assess passengers' mental readiness for space travel.

*Children and Families*
Are children allowed on the trip?
Yes, children are allowed on the trip, but they must meet specific age and health requirements. We provide family-friendly accommodations and activities, including educational programs and recreational areas designed for children. We also have a dedicated team of child care professionals.
What are the child care facilities?
We offer comprehensive child care facilities and activities on board the spacecraft, including educational programs, recreational areas, and supervised play areas. Our child care professionals are trained to provide a safe and stimulating environment for children of all ages.
What are the family activities?
We provide a range of family activities, including guided tours of Mars, educational workshops, and recreational games. We aim to create a memorable and enriching experience for families traveling to Mars. We also offer family-friendly dining options and entertainment.
